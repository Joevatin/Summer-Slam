Tournament Details

Standard basketball rules will apply, with the following notes

Rules:

  • There will be zero tolerance for bad language, confrontations (both verbal and physical) and arguing with or disrespecting the referees calls.
  • Any player can be kicked out of the tournament, without compensation at the referees or tournament directors discretion. Each player will get 1 warning at best for any infraction before they will be asked to leave.
  • Each team will play a minimum of 3 season games.
  • All teams will make the playoffs, which will begin single elimination rounds.
  • All teams must have a minimum of 7 players and a maximum of 10 players.
  • Each game will be a timed 20 minute game.
  • Each team will have one (1) 30 sec timeout per game during the season games. During the championship game each team will have two (2) timeouts.

Scoring:

  • 1 point for shots inside the 3-point arc.
  • 2 points for shots outside the 3-point arc.

Out of Bounds:

  • The ball gets checked in wherever it went out of bounds.

Fouls:

  • Each player will be allowed 4 fouls per game. Upon reaching 4 fouls the player will be eliminated from the current game.
  • There are no free throws.
  • If you are fouled while attempting a shot and you make the shot you will be awarded the point but there will be no free throw and a foul will be counted against the fouler.
  • If you are fouled while attempting a shot and you miss the shot you will be awarded possession of the ball and a foul will be counted against the fouler.
  • Fouls during play result in possession at the top of the key and foul counted against fouler.

Delay of Game:

  • If a player is injured to the point of stopping play then the player must substitute out of play.
  • Players are encouraged not to intentionally hold the ball to waste the Game Clock.
  • Backcourt violations result in a change of possession.
